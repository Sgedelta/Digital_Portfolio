Name

Hearder

Primary Action

Primary Action of the Game is protecting your "flock" of agents as they navigate the world, you do so by getting rid of threats and spawning them food

Genres

Genres:

  • Simulation
  • Survival
  • Resource Management

Platform

Desktop Only, with a stretch goal of being Mobile Friendly

Story

Protect your flock from the predators!

Theme is Survival, Mood is retro-inspired, narrative and premise are the same - protect your flock from the predators.

Aesthetics

Simple Graphics, 8-bit esqu audio that occurs with most actions and as background

Gameplay

Mechanics are simple: A way to remove predators (probably via clicking on them) and a way to spawn food (via a button). There also may be a SMALL way to control your flock, but they are mostly out of your hands
